Our Services

Absko Advocates is a 21st century law firm based in Nairobi, Kenya. It was established in 2002 by Mr. Barasa Chege Chilemba (Exq) with offices in Nairobi and Mombasa. Absko Advocates Law is made up of 8 Partners and over 16 lawyers with expertise in a wide variety of legal matters, with a well-established Areas of Practice and focus on Litigation, Business & Contract Law, Company Law & Corporate Advisory, Constitutional & Administrative Law, Election Law & Electoral Advisory, Employment & Labour Relations Law, Family & Divorce Law, Intellectual Property, International Trade & Investment, Law of Succession: Wills, Probate & Estate Administration, Real Estate, Conveyancing & Land Law. As one of the Top Law Firms in Kenya, we are committed to providing the best legal services.

The team at Absko Advocates, comprising some of the Best Lawyers in Kenya, has the right blend of legal and sectoral Areas of Practice as well as a proven record in litigation and commercial law practice.

The Absko Advocates team shares a commitment to providing our clients with the highest quality and most cost-effective legal services in an atmosphere emphasizing teamwork, creativity, responsiveness and diversity. This commitment combined with our in-depth knowledge and experience in each of our practice areas allows us to deliver the highest level of service.
